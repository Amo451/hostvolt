How We Test

Our Review Methodology

Every host we recommend has been through our full testing process. Here's exactly what we measure.

⏱️
60-Day Hands-On Testing
We run real WordPress sites on every host for at least 60 days before publishing any review — not just a weekend test.
📡
Third-Party Uptime Monitoring
We use UptimeRobot to track uptime every 5 minutes from multiple locations throughout the testing period.
⚡
Real Speed Tests
Load times are measured using GTmetrix and Pingdom from servers in North America, Europe, and Asia.
💬
Support Quality Tests
We contact every host's support team multiple times with real questions and score them on response time and quality.

About HostVault

We Test So You Don't Have To

HostVault was started in 2022 after we got burned by a hosting provider that looked great on paper but delivered terrible performance in practice. We couldn't find a review site that actually tested hosts properly — so we built one.

Every recommendation on this site comes from hands-on testing. We pay for hosting accounts ourselves, run real websites on them, and measure what actually matters. We never accept payment to change or improve a rating.

When you buy through our links we may earn a commission — that's how we keep the lights on. But it has never and will never influence our ratings.
